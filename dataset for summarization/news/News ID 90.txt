Headline:
Government Announces Nationwide Battery Recycling Program to Support Clean Energy Transition

News Content:
The Ministry of Environment and the Ministry of Energy have jointly launched a nationwide battery recycling initiative to improve the safe collection and recycling of used lithium-ion, lead-acid, and other rechargeable batteries. The program includes certified recycling centers, public awareness campaigns, and incentives for consumers and businesses to dispose of batteries responsibly. Environmental experts believe the initiative will reduce hazardous waste, recover valuable raw materials, and support the sustainable growth of electric vehicles and renewable energy technologies.
