Headline:
Government Introduces AI-Based Crop Disease Detection System for Farmers

News Content:
The Ministry of Agriculture has launched an Artificial Intelligence (AI)-powered crop disease detection system to help farmers identify plant diseases at an early stage. The platform uses drone imagery, mobile applications, and image recognition technology to analyze crop health and recommend appropriate treatments. Agricultural experts stated that early disease detection will reduce crop losses, improve productivity, and lower farming costs while supporting sustainable agricultural practices.
