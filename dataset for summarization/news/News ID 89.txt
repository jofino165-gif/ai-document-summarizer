Headline:
National Railway Introduces AI-Based Predictive Maintenance to Reduce Train Delays

News Content:
The National Railway Authority has deployed an Artificial Intelligence (AI)-based predictive maintenance system across key railway corridors. The system analyzes real-time sensor data from trains and railway tracks to identify potential equipment failures before they occur. Officials stated that the technology will improve railway safety, reduce unexpected breakdowns, minimize train delays, and lower long-term maintenance costs while enhancing passenger reliability.
