Headline:
Hospitals Report Sharp Increase in Dengue Cases Following Continuous Rainfall

News Content:
Health authorities have reported a significant rise in dengue fever cases across several districts after weeks of continuous rainfall created favorable conditions for mosquito breeding. Hospitals have recorded an increase in patients with high fever, severe headache, muscle pain, skin rashes, and low platelet counts. Public health officials have intensified mosquito control measures and urged residents to eliminate stagnant water around homes and workplaces.
