Government Launches Nationwide Rainwater Harvesting Initiative to Address Water Scarcity

News Content:
The Ministry of Water Resources has introduced a nationwide rainwater harvesting initiative to improve water conservation and reduce dependence on groundwater. The program provides financial incentives and technical support for households, schools, industries, and commercial buildings to install rainwater harvesting systems. Environmental experts believe the initiative will improve groundwater recharge, increase water availability during dry seasons, and strengthen long-term water security.
