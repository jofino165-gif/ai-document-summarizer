Headline:
Government Introduces AI-Powered Water Quality Monitoring System for Rivers and Lakes

News Content:
The Ministry of Water Resources has launched an Artificial Intelligence (AI)-powered water quality monitoring system to continuously assess the condition of rivers, lakes, and reservoirs across the country. The system uses smart sensors, satellite imagery, and real-time data analytics to detect pollution, monitor water quality parameters, and identify contamination risks at an early stage. Environmental experts stated that the initiative will improve water resource management, protect public health, and support sustainable environmental conservation.
