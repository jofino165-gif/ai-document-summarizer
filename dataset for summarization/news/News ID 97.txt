Government Launches AI-Based Air Quality Monitoring Network Across Major Cities

News Content:
The Ministry of Environment has introduced an Artificial Intelligence (AI)-powered air quality monitoring network in major cities to improve pollution tracking and environmental management. Environmental experts believe the initiative will support faster policy decisions, improve public health awareness, and reduce the long-term impact of air pollution.
