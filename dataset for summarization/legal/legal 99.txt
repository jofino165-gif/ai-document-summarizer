# Legal Document 99 – AI Model Training Data License Agreement

### Original Text

An AI Model Training Data License Agreement is a legally binding contract between a data licensor and a data licensee that establishes the terms and conditions for licensing datasets used to train, validate, fine-tune, and evaluate artificial intelligence and machine learning models. The agreement defines the scope of the data license, permitted and prohibited uses, intellectual property rights, confidentiality obligations, data security requirements, payment terms, compliance obligations, warranty limitations, liability provisions, termination conditions, and dispute resolution mechanisms. AI Model Training Data License Agreements are commonly used by businesses, research organizations, healthcare institutions, financial organizations, educational institutions, government agencies, technology companies, and AI developers to ensure lawful access to high-quality datasets while protecting the legal, technical, and commercial interests of both parties. The agreement provides a structured legal framework that governs responsible use of licensed datasets throughout the licensing period.

The agreement begins by identifying the data licensor and the data licensee, including their legal names, registered business addresses, and authorized representatives. It specifies the effective date of the agreement, the duration of the license, and a detailed description of the licensed datasets. These datasets may include text corpora, image collections, audio recordings, video datasets, structured databases, sensor data, medical datasets, financial records, geospatial information, annotated datasets, or synthetic datasets. The agreement identifies the permitted purposes of data usage, authorized users, storage requirements, geographic restrictions, access controls, and any limitations regarding redistribution or sublicensing. Clearly defining the scope of the data license helps establish expectations and reduces misunderstandings regarding lawful dataset usage.

One of the most important sections of the AI Model Training Data License Agreement outlines the responsibilities of the data licensee. The licensee is granted a non-exclusive, non-transferable, and revocable license to access and use the licensed datasets solely for the purposes authorized under the agreement. The licensee agrees not to redistribute, resell, disclose, modify, reverse engineer, sublicense, or otherwise exploit the datasets except as expressly permitted by the licensor or required by applicable law. The licensee is responsible for implementing appropriate security controls, protecting access credentials, restricting unauthorized access, maintaining audit records, and ensuring compliance with all licensing conditions.

The agreement also specifies the responsibilities of the data licensor. The licensor agrees to provide access to the licensed datasets, associated documentation, metadata, and any updates included under the agreement. The licensor is responsible for ensuring that it possesses the legal authority to license the datasets, maintaining the confidentiality of customer information, providing reasonable technical support where applicable, and promptly notifying the licensee of significant changes affecting dataset availability or licensing terms.

Financial provisions clearly define the commercial terms of the agreement. The document specifies licensing fees, subscription charges where applicable, billing schedules, payment methods, applicable taxes, renewal fees, reimbursement of approved expenses, and charges for optional services such as additional dataset access, annotation services, custom dataset preparation, technical support, consulting services, or expanded usage rights. Clearly documenting financial obligations helps reduce payment disputes and promotes transparent commercial relationships.

Confidentiality provisions require both parties to protect confidential information exchanged during the data licensing relationship. Confidential information may include licensed datasets, metadata, annotation guidelines, customer records, employee information, financial reports, authentication credentials, technical documentation, research information, business strategies, proprietary technologies, and intellectual property. Neither party may disclose confidential information to unauthorized individuals without prior written consent unless disclosure is required by law. These confidentiality obligations generally remain effective after the agreement has expired or been terminated.

The agreement also establishes intellectual property ownership and compliance requirements. The data licensor retains full ownership of the licensed datasets, associated documentation, copyrights, database rights, trademarks where applicable, and all related intellectual property rights. The license granted under the agreement does not transfer ownership of the datasets to the licensee. The licensor may conduct reasonable compliance audits to verify that the datasets are being used in accordance with the agreement, subject to reasonable notice and applicable legal requirements.

Termination provisions explain the circumstances under which either party may terminate the AI Model Training Data License Agreement. Reasons for termination may include material breach of contract, unauthorized dataset usage, non-payment, insolvency, violation of confidentiality obligations, unlawful processing activities, or mutual written agreement. Upon termination, the licensee agrees to discontinue all use of the licensed datasets, securely delete or return all licensed data where required, certify compliance with the termination requirements if requested, and return confidential information. The licensor agrees to discontinue data access following termination.

Finally, the AI Model Training Data License Agreement includes provisions regarding governing law, dispute resolution, limitation of liability, audit rights, amendment procedures, legal compliance, warranty disclaimers, and force majeure. If disputes arise concerning dataset licensing rights, payment obligations, confidentiality requirements, intellectual property ownership, authorized data usage, or interpretation of the agreement, the parties agree to first attempt negotiation or mediation before pursuing arbitration or court proceedings. By clearly defining data licensing rights, user responsibilities, financial obligations, confidentiality protections, intellectual property ownership, compliance requirements, termination conditions, and dispute resolution mechanisms, an AI Model Training Data License Agreement provides a comprehensive legal framework that supports lawful, secure, and responsible use of datasets for artificial intelligence and machine learning model development while protecting the legal, technical, and commercial interests of both the data licensor and the data licensee.
