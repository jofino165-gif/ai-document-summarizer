# Legal Document 95 – AI Data Processing Agreement

### Original Text

An AI Data Processing Agreement is a legally binding contract between a data controller and an AI data processor that establishes the terms and conditions for collecting, processing, storing, analyzing, transferring, and protecting data used in artificial intelligence systems. The agreement defines the scope of data processing activities, responsibilities of both parties, data security requirements, confidentiality obligations, compliance with privacy regulations, intellectual property rights, payment terms, audit rights, data retention policies, and dispute resolution mechanisms. AI Data Processing Agreements are commonly used by businesses, healthcare organizations, financial institutions, educational institutions, government agencies, research organizations, cloud service providers, and technology companies to ensure lawful and secure processing of data for AI applications while protecting the legal, technical, and commercial interests of both parties. The agreement provides a structured legal framework that supports responsible data governance and compliance with applicable data protection laws.

The agreement begins by identifying the data controller and the AI data processor, including their legal names, registered business addresses, and authorized representatives. It specifies the effective date of the agreement, the duration of data processing activities, and a detailed description of the categories of personal data, business data, operational data, research data, images, audio recordings, text documents, sensor data, and other information covered under the agreement. The agreement also identifies the purposes of processing, lawful bases for processing, permitted processing activities, approved storage locations, international data transfer conditions, and authorized personnel who may access the data. Clearly defining the scope of data processing helps establish accountability and regulatory compliance.

One of the most important sections of the AI Data Processing Agreement outlines the responsibilities of the AI data processor. The processor agrees to process data only according to documented instructions provided by the data controller, implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures, maintain accurate processing records, protect confidential information, assist with regulatory compliance activities, support data subject rights requests, report data breaches without undue delay, and securely delete or return data upon completion of processing activities. The processor is responsible for ensuring that authorized personnel receive appropriate privacy and security training and comply with all contractual obligations.

The agreement also specifies the responsibilities of the data controller. The controller agrees to provide lawful instructions for processing activities, ensure that data has been collected in accordance with applicable laws, maintain appropriate legal bases for processing, provide accurate processing requirements, cooperate during compliance reviews, respond to regulatory inquiries, and make payments according to the agreed financial schedule. The controller is responsible for obtaining required consents where applicable and ensuring that AI processing activities comply with applicable privacy legislation. Effective cooperation between both parties supports responsible data processing.

Financial provisions clearly define the commercial terms of the agreement. The document specifies processing service fees, recurring subscription charges where applicable, billing schedules, payment methods, applicable taxes, reimbursement of approved expenses, and charges for additional services beyond the agreed scope. Additional services may include compliance consulting, security assessments, international transfer support, advanced data anonymization, privacy impact assessments, audit assistance, or specialized reporting services. Clearly documenting financial obligations helps reduce payment disputes and supports transparent commercial relationships.

Confidentiality provisions require both parties to protect confidential information exchanged during the AI data processing engagement. Confidential information may include personal data, customer records, employee information, healthcare records, financial information, authentication credentials, encryption keys, technical documentation, software source code, business strategies, research information, proprietary technologies, and intellectual property. Neither party may disclose confidential information to unauthorized individuals without prior written consent unless disclosure is required by law. These confidentiality obligations generally remain effective after the agreement has expired or been terminated.

The agreement also establishes data protection and regulatory compliance requirements. Both parties agree to comply with applicable privacy, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, consumer protection, and data governance laws throughout the processing lifecycle. The processor agrees to implement encryption, access controls, audit logging, secure storage, incident response procedures, and periodic security assessments to protect processed information. Where required by law, the processor agrees to support regulatory inspections and provide documentation demonstrating compliance with contractual and legal obligations.

Termination provisions explain the circumstances under which either party may terminate the AI Data Processing Agreement. Reasons for termination may include material breach of contract, unlawful processing activities, non-payment, insolvency, violation of confidentiality obligations, regulatory non-compliance, or mutual written agreement. Upon termination, the processor agrees to discontinue all processing activities, return or securely delete processed data where legally permitted, provide documentation confirming data disposition, return confidential information, and assist with orderly transition activities if requested.

Finally, the AI Data Processing Agreement includes provisions regarding governing law, dispute resolution, limitation of liability, audit rights, amendment procedures, compliance reviews, security reporting, and periodic performance evaluations. If disputes arise concerning data processing activities, payment obligations, confidentiality requirements, regulatory compliance, intellectual property ownership, or interpretation of the agreement, the parties agree to first attempt negotiation or mediation before pursuing arbitration or court proceedings. By clearly defining data processing responsibilities, security requirements, financial obligations, confidentiality protections, compliance measures, termination conditions, and dispute resolution mechanisms, an AI Data Processing Agreement provides a comprehensive legal framework that supports lawful, secure, and responsible processing of data for artificial intelligence systems while protecting the legal, technical, and commercial interests of both the data controller and the AI data processor.
