# Legal Document 97 – AI Consulting Services Agreement

### Original Text

An AI Consulting Services Agreement is a legally binding contract between a client and an artificial intelligence consulting service provider that establishes the terms and conditions for providing professional consulting, advisory, strategy, implementation planning, and technical guidance related to artificial intelligence technologies. The agreement defines the scope of consulting services, responsibilities of both parties, project objectives, deliverables, confidentiality obligations, intellectual property rights, payment terms, compliance requirements, reporting obligations, and dispute resolution mechanisms. AI Consulting Services Agreements are commonly used by businesses, healthcare organizations, financial institutions, educational institutions, government agencies, manufacturing companies, startups, and technology enterprises seeking expert guidance on adopting and implementing AI solutions. The agreement provides a structured legal framework that supports successful AI initiatives while protecting the legal, technical, and commercial interests of both parties.

The agreement begins by identifying the client and the AI consulting service provider, including their legal names, registered business addresses, and authorized representatives. It specifies the effective date of the agreement, the duration of the consulting engagement, and a detailed description of the consulting services to be provided. These services may include AI readiness assessments, digital transformation planning, feasibility studies, AI strategy development, machine learning implementation planning, technology selection, risk assessments, regulatory compliance guidance, project roadmaps, and organizational training. The agreement identifies project objectives, expected deliverables, reporting schedules, consultation methods, implementation timelines, and communication procedures. Clearly defining the consulting scope helps establish expectations and minimizes misunderstandings during the engagement.

One of the most important sections of the AI Consulting Services Agreement outlines the responsibilities of the consulting service provider. The provider agrees to evaluate the client's business requirements, assess existing technology infrastructure, recommend appropriate AI solutions, prepare technical reports, provide implementation guidance, conduct workshops, support strategic decision-making, and deliver consulting services according to recognized professional standards. The provider is responsible for maintaining accurate project documentation, providing objective recommendations, protecting confidential information, and completing agreed deliverables within established timelines.

The agreement also specifies the responsibilities of the client. The client agrees to provide accurate business information, technical documentation, operational requirements, timely feedback, authorized access to relevant personnel, and necessary resources required for successful consulting activities. The client is responsible for reviewing consulting reports, participating in planning sessions, making strategic decisions regarding AI implementation, and making payments according to the agreed financial schedule. Effective collaboration between both parties contributes to successful consulting outcomes.

Financial provisions clearly define the commercial terms of the agreement. The document specifies consulting fees, milestone-based payments where applicable, billing schedules, payment methods, applicable taxes, reimbursement of approved expenses, and charges for additional consulting services beyond the agreed scope. Additional services may include implementation support, advanced technical assessments, executive workshops, employee training, regulatory advisory services, vendor evaluations, or post-engagement consulting. Clearly documenting financial obligations helps reduce payment disputes and supports transparent project management.

Confidentiality provisions require both parties to protect confidential information exchanged during the consulting engagement. Confidential information may include business strategies, AI implementation plans, technical documentation, customer records, employee information, financial reports, authentication credentials, research information, software source code, proprietary technologies, intellectual property, and operational data. Neither party may disclose confidential information to unauthorized individuals without prior written consent unless disclosure is required by law. These confidentiality obligations generally remain effective after the agreement has expired or been terminated.

The agreement also establishes intellectual property ownership and professional advisory standards. Unless otherwise agreed in writing, the client retains ownership of project-specific deliverables, reports, implementation roadmaps, and documentation prepared under the agreement upon full payment. The consulting service provider retains ownership of pre-existing consulting methodologies, analytical frameworks, reusable templates, proprietary assessment tools, and independently developed technologies unless specifically assigned under the agreement. Both parties agree to comply with applicable laws governing artificial intelligence, privacy, cybersecurity, intellectual property, and professional consulting practices throughout the engagement.

Termination provisions explain the circumstances under which either party may terminate the AI Consulting Services Agreement. Reasons for termination may include material breach of contract, repeated failure to perform agreed consulting services, non-payment, insolvency, violation of confidentiality obligations, or mutual written agreement. Upon termination, the consulting service provider agrees to deliver completed consulting work, return confidential information, transfer agreed documentation where applicable, securely remove retained client information where legally permitted, and provide reasonable transition assistance according to the agreement.

Finally, the AI Consulting Services Agreement includes provisions regarding governing law, dispute resolution, limitation of liability, legal compliance, audit rights, amendment procedures, project reporting, quality assurance, and periodic engagement reviews. If disputes arise concerning consulting services, payment obligations, confidentiality requirements, intellectual property ownership, consulting deliverables, or interpretation of the agreement, the parties agree to first attempt negotiation or mediation before pursuing arbitration or court proceedings. By clearly defining consulting responsibilities, project deliverables, financial obligations, confidentiality protections, intellectual property rights, professional advisory standards, termination conditions, and dispute resolution mechanisms, an AI Consulting Services Agreement provides a comprehensive legal framework that supports successful AI consulting engagements while protecting the legal, technical, and commercial interests of both the client and the AI consulting service provider.
