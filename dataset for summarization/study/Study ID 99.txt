Topic:
Data Science

Uploaded Notes:
Data Science is an interdisciplinary field that combines statistics, mathematics, computer science, and domain knowledge to extract meaningful insights from structured and unstructured data. It focuses on analyzing data to support decision-making and solve real-world problems.

Data Science involves multiple stages including data collection, data cleaning, data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), modeling, evaluation, and visualization. Each stage helps transform raw data into actionable insights.

Key components of Data Science include statistics, data analysis, machine learning, data engineering, and data visualization. These components work together to understand patterns and build predictive models.

Common tools and technologies used in Data Science include Python, R, SQL, Jupyter Notebook, Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, Tableau, and Power BI.

Data Science is widely used in industries such as healthcare, finance, marketing, e-commerce, transportation, and social media for prediction, classification, recommendation systems, and trend analysis.

The advantages of Data Science include better decision-making, predictive capabilities, automation of analysis, and improved business strategies. Challenges include data quality issues, privacy concerns, high computational requirements, and complex data integration.

Data Science is the process of extracting insights and knowledge from data using scientific methods, algorithms, and systems.
